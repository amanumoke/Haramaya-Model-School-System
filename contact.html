Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

Admission requires completion of the previous grade level with satisfactory performance, submission of academic records, and passing our entrance examination. Please visit our registration page for full details.

Fees vary by grade level and include tuition, textbooks, and extracurricular activities. We offer installment payment plans and financial aid for qualifying students. For specific fee information, please contact our admissions office.

We offer sports (football, basketball, athletics), academic clubs (science, debate, math), arts (music, drama, painting), and community service programs. Activities vary by grade level and season.

Tours are available Monday-Friday during school hours. Please contact our office at least 24 hours in advance to schedule. Group tours for prospective students are also held monthly - check our calendar for dates.

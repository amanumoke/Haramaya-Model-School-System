About Our School

35 years of excellence in education

Our History

Founded in 1985, Haramaya Model High School has grown from humble beginnings to become a regional leader in education.

What started as a small school with just 50 students has now blossomed into a premier educational institution serving over 800 students annually. Our commitment to academic excellence and character development has remained unchanged throughout our history.

Over the years, we've expanded our facilities, developed innovative teaching methods, and built a strong alumni network that spans across Ethiopia and beyond.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that equips students with knowledge, skills, and values to succeed in higher education and contribute meaningfully to society.

Vision

To be recognized as a model institution that develops well-rounded, innovative, and ethical leaders who will drive positive change in Ethiopia and beyond.

Our Core Values
